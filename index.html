Hi, my name is Paige


I am a computer scientist, artist, skier, coffee aficionado, video game enthusiast, and a huge proponent of sweatpants. I will received my B.S. in Computer Science from the from the University of Colorado in Boulder in May of 2017. Afterwards you will find me water-skiing down in Arizona, backpacking around Europe, and interning with the Xbox team at Microsoft.

My Passion in Technology

I grew up on a Cattle Ranch on the beautiful plains of Colorado. When I wasn't herding cows, I was welding cool pieces of metal together or tinkering with the computer. Even from a young age, I was fascinated with technology. I've always enjoyed the intersection of people, art, and technology.

Finding What I Have to Offer

"Life consists in learning to live on one’s own, spontaneous, freewheeling: to do this one must recognize what is one’s own—be familiar and at home with oneself. This means basically learning who one is, and learning what one has to offer to the contemporary world, and then learning how to make that offering valid." -Thomas Merton

It can be easy to miss the beauty in all of the moments we are given in life. I love finding joy in the people that I'm surrounded by, and trying to bring that same joy to others. I live everyday striving to find what I have to offer the people around me and how I can live that out.
