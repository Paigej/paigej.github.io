For this assignment, I am going to try to make a mold for a mold for a mold for the "Spyro the Dragon" Dragon crystal. (on thingaverse here).


.


The first thing I did with this model was remove the wings, and create joints where they can snap in. The model felt too complex with the wings to come out cleanly.



Using the dice seam example as inspiration, I tried creating a surface that I could use to make the seams on the mold be on the corners of the polygon dragon. Unfortunately, I couldn't get both boolean difference and boolean split to work. I gave up at about three in the morning and decided to move forward just splitting the dragon down the middle.



I'm splitting the dragon in half to place it in a box for a bold, with pour spouts and vents. I need to place the vents more careful and explore possibly putting vents on each foot.
